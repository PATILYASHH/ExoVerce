About Exoverse

From Sage Universe Org

Exoverse is a revolutionary virtual electronics lab developed by Sage Universe Org. It enables users to build, experiment, and innovate with real-world physics and beyond. Designed for engineers, students, and tech enthusiasts, Exoverse brings the power of a real electronics lab into a digital space.

Features

  • Simulate real-world electronic circuits
  • Build and test prototypes with customizable components
  • Modify the laws of physics to create futuristic inventions
  • Collaborate in a shared virtual workspace
  • Support for multiple programming languages
  • 3D designing tools for complex system creation

Future Vision

Exoverse aims to redefine how electronics and innovation are approached. In the future, users will be able to integrate AI-driven assistance, real-time collaboration tools, and even order physical prototypes of their virtual designs.
