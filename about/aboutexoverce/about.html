Welcome to Exoverce

The ultimate virtual electronics lab for innovators, creators, and engineers.

What is Exoverce?

Exoverce is a virtual electronics lab where users can create, innovate, and build machines from real-world systems to futuristic or even impossible creations. From coding robots to designing complex systems like rockets, Exoverce allows you to experiment with limitless possibilities.

Features

Build Anything

Design, wire, and code systems just like you would in a physical lab. Create robots, machines, cars, and even rockets.

Customize Real-World Laws

Modify the laws of nature and experiment with futuristic or impossible designs in the lab.

Collaboration

Collaborate with other users in multi-user labs to work together on exciting projects.
